Translation

Translation is the conversion of text from one language to another while preserving its meaning. It's used for effective communication across languages in various contexts, but capturing nuances and context can be challenging..

Transliteration

Transliteration is the process of converting text from one script or alphabet into another, while keeping the pronunciation and phonetic sounds intact. In transliteration, the focus is on representing the sounds of words rather than their meanings.
